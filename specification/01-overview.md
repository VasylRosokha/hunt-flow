# 1. Overview

**HuntFlow** is a full-stack web application that helps job seekers track their applications, get AI-powered insights from job descriptions, generate tailored cover letters, and visualize their job search progress through an analytics dashboard.

## Target User

Developers and tech professionals actively searching for jobs, especially those applying to multiple positions across different platforms.

## What Makes It Different

Unlike Notion templates or bloated SaaS tools, HuntFlow is a focused, fast, open-source app built for developers by a developer. The AI doesn't just generate generic text — it analyzes job descriptions against YOUR specific skills and gives actionable advice.
